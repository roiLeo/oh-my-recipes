title: 'Gingerbread Cookies'
description: "Classic Christmas gingerbread cookies with warm spices and a soft, chewy texture. Perfect for decorating, gifting, or enjoying with hot cocoa during the holiday season."
publishedAt: 2025-12-24
category: 'Dessert'
prepTime: 20
cookTime: 12
servings: 24
difficulty: 'medium'
image: 'https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1639590229762-3c9a8c98ca31?w=900&auto=format&fit=crop&q=60&ixlib=rb-4.1.0&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxzZWFyY2h8M3x8Z2luZ2VyYnJlYWR8ZW58MHx8MHx8fDA%3D()
imageAlt: 'Decorated gingerbread cookies with white icing'
ingredients:
  - '2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour'
  - '1 teaspoon baking soda'
  - '1/2 teaspoon salt'
  - '1 tablespoon ground ginger'
  - '2 teaspoons ground cinnamon'
  - '1/4 teaspoon ground cloves'
  - '1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg'
  - '3/4 cup (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened'
  - '1/2 cup brown sugar, packed'
  - '1/2 cup molasses'
  - '1 large egg'
  - '1 teaspoon vanilla extract'
instructions:
  - title: 'Prepare the Dough'
    steps:
      - text: 'In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg. Set aside.'
      - text: 'In a large bowl, cream together softened butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.'
        timer: 180
      - text: 'Beat in molasses, egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.'
      - text: 'Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until just combined. Do not overmix.'
  - title: 'Chill and Cut'
    steps:
      - text: 'Divide dough in half and shape into disks. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.'
        timer: 1800
      - text: 'Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.'
      - text: 'On a floured surface, roll out dough to 1/4-inch thickness.'
      - text: 'Use cookie cutters to cut into festive shapes (gingerbread men, stars, snowflakes, etc.).'
      - text: 'Place cookies 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets.'
  - title: 'Bake'
    steps:
      - text: 'Bake for 10-12 minutes until the edges are set but centers are still slightly soft.'
        timer: 660
      - text: 'Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 3 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.'
        timer: 180
      - text: 'Cool completely before decorating.'
  - title: 'Decorate'
    steps:
      - text: 'Once cooled, decorate with royal icing, buttercream, or simple powdered sugar icing if desired.'
      - text: 'Add sprinkles, candies, or edible glitter for a festive touch.'
      - text: 'Let icing dry completely before storing.'
  - title: 'Enjoy'
    steps:
      - text: 'Store in an airtight container for up to one week. Perfect for holiday gift bags or enjoying with family and friends!'
